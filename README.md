## The four natures of emergence

The legend spans all four, from goddess to Guardian:

- 🟢 **natural** — mortals, beasts, land-guardians *(Ganondorf, Impa, the Great Deku Tree, Epona)*
- 🟣 **ethereal** — fairies, sword-spirits, the Twilight *(Fi, Midna, Navi)*
- 🟡 **spiritual** — the goddesses, the sacred relics, the cycle, the curse *(Link, Zelda, the Triforce, the Master Sword, the Goddesses, Demise)*
- 🔵 **electrical** — the ancient Sheikah machines *(the Guardians & Divine Beasts)*
